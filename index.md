## 🛡️ Best Practices

* Always create a backup of your device data before you start any service operation. Write operations may erase user data.
* Keep your laptop plugged into a power source during the entire process. A sudden shutdown during a flash operation can cause damage to the device firmware.
* Use original USB cables to maintain a stable connection between the phone and the PC.
* Close other background programs that use USB ports to prevent connection errors during the process.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if the software does not detect my phone?
Verify that you have installed the correct Qualcomm drivers. Check that your cable works with other devices. Make sure that your phone is in the correct mode for service.

Does this tool work on Windows 7 or 8?
This version builds specifically for Windows 10 and Windows 11. Use a newer operating system for optimal stability.

Will this tool unlock my phone carrier?
This tool provides low-level device access. While it supports service operations, carrier unlocking depends on the specific security settings of your device firmware and the regional requirements of your service provider.

Can I use this on non-Qualcomm phones?
No. This software communicates exclusively with devices that contain a Qualcomm chipset. If you use it with other chipsets, the software will not recognize the connection.

How do I uninstall the tool?
Open the Control Panel on your Windows computer. Select Programs and Features. Find the QPST entry in the list, right-click it, and select Uninstall. Follow the prompts to remove the files from your system.
